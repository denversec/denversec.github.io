About

DenverSec is just one of the many local CitySec meet-ups around the world. CitySec was spawned out of /r/NetSec as a way to bring together local communities with a shared interest in information security.

The Denver meet-up is designed specifically to be a social gathering. There are no presentations or slide decks and sales pitches are discouraged. All are welcome -- even if you're not working in the industry!

Meet-Up

We usually meet on the third Wednesday of each month at a downtown location. The dates and locations can sometimes vary, but they always start at 7:00 PM. Be sure to follow our Twitter account, @DenverSec, to keep up with the upcoming meet-ups and related announcements.

We've now added a subscribable iCal calendar URL that you can use to keep track of upcoming events!
