About CitySec

Denver CitySec is just one of the many local CitySec meetups around the country. CitySec was spawned out of /r/NetSec as a way to bring together local communities with the shared interest in information security.

The Denver meetup is designed specifically to be a social gathering. There are no presentations or slide decks and sales pitches are discouraged.

Meetups

We meet on the third Wednesday of each month at a downtown location. The locations can vary, but they always start at 7:00 PM.

Be sure to follow our Twitter account, @DenverSec, to keep up with upcoming meetups.
