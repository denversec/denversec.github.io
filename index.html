About

Denver CitySec is just one of the many local CitySec meetups around the world. CitySec was spawned out of /r/NetSec as a way to bring together local communities with a shared interest in information security.

The Denver meetup is designed specifically to be a social gathering. There are no presentations or slide decks and sales pitches are discouraged. All are welcome -- even if you're not in the industry!

Meetup

We meet on the third Wednesday of each month at a downtown location. The locations can vary, but they always start at 7:00 PM. Be sure to follow our Twitter account, @DenverSec, to keep up with upcoming meetups and related announcements.
